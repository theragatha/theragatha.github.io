# 1.36 Kumāputta

“Sādhu sutaṁ sādhu caritakaṁ,\
Sādhu sadā aniketavihāro;\
Atthapucchanaṁ padakkhiṇakammaṁ,\
Etaṁ sāmaññamakiñcanassā”ti.
