# 1.38 Gavampati

“Yo iddhiyā sarabhuṁ aṭṭhapesi,\
So gavampati asito anejo;\
Taṁ sabbasaṅgātigataṁ mahāmuniṁ,\
Devā namassanti bhavassa pāragun”ti.
