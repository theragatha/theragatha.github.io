# 1.39 Tissa

“Sattiyā viya omaṭṭho,\
ḍayhamānova matthake;[^1]\
Kāmarāgappahānāya,\
sato bhikkhu paribbaje”ti.

## Attribution

… Tisso thero ….

## Notes

[^1]: Variant: ḍayhamānova → ḍayhamāneva (sabbattha)
