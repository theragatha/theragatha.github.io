# 1.66 Meghiya

“Anusāsi mahāvīro,\
sabbadhammāna pāragū;\
Tassāhaṁ dhammaṁ sutvāna,\
vihāsiṁ santike sato;[^1]\
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,\
kataṁ buddhassa sāsanan”ti.

## Attribution

… Meghiyo thero ….

## Notes

[^1]: Variant: sato → rato (sya1ed, sya2ed, pts1ed)
