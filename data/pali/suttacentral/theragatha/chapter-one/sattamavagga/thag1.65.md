# 1.65 Ukkhepakatavaccha [^1]

“Ukkhepakatavacchassa,\
Saṅkalitaṁ bahūhi vassehi;\
Taṁ bhāsati gahaṭṭhānaṁ,\
Sunisinno uḷārapāmojjo”ti.

## Attribution

… Ukkhepakatavaccho thero ….[^2]
