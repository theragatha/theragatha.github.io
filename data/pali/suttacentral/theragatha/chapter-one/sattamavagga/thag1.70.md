# 1.70 Puṇṇattheragāthā
“Sīlameva idha aggaṁ,
paññavā pana uttamo;
Manussesu ca devesu,
sīlapaññāṇato jayan”ti.

## Attribution

… Puṇṇo thero …

## Notes

vaggo sattamo.

Tassuddānaṁ

Vappo ca vajjiputto ca,
pakkho vimalakoṇḍañño;
Ukkhepakatavaccho ca,
meghiyo ekadhammiko;
Ekudāniyachannā ca,
puṇṇatthero mahabbaloti.
