# 1.43 Sumaṅgala

“Sumuttiko sumuttiko,\
Sāhu sumuttikomhi tīhi khujjakehi;\
Asitāsu mayā naṅgalāsu mayā,\
Khuddakuddālāsu mayā.\
Yadipi idhameva idhameva,\
Atha vāpi alameva alameva;\
Jhāya sumaṅgala jhāya sumaṅgala,\
Appamatto vihara sumaṅgalā”ti.
