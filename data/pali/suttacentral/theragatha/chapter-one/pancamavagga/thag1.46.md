# 1.46 Samiddhittheragāthā

“Saddhāyāhaṁ pabbajito,\
Agārasmānagāriyaṁ;\
Sati paññā ca me vuḍḍhā,\
Cittañca susamāhitaṁ;\
Kāmaṁ karassu rūpāni,\
Neva maṁ byādhayissasī”ti.[^1]
