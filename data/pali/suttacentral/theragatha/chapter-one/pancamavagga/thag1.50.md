# 1.50 Vimala

“Dharaṇī ca siñcati vāti,\
Māluto vijjutā carati nabhe;\
Upasamanti vitakkā,\
Cittaṁ susamāhitaṁ mamā”ti.

## Attribution

… Vimalo thero …

## Notes

vaggo pañcamo.

Tassuddānaṁ

Sirīvaḍḍho revato thero,
sumaṅgalo sānusavhayo;
Ramaṇīyavihārī ca,
samiddhiujjayasañjayā;
Rāmaṇeyyo ca so thero,
vimalo ca raṇañjahoti.
