# 1.55 Añjanavaniya

“Āsandiṁ kuṭikaṁ katvā,\
ogayha añjanaṁ vanaṁ;\
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,\
kataṁ buddhassa sāsanan”ti.

## Attribution

… Añjanavaniyo thero ….[^1]

## Notes

[^1]: Variant: Añjanavaniyo → añjanāvaniyo (sya1ed, sya2ed, pts1ed, pts2ed)
