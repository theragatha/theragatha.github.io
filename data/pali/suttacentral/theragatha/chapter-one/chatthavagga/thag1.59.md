# 1.59 Kosalavihāri

“Saddhāyāhaṁ pabbajito,\
araññe me kuṭikā katā;\
Appamatto ca ātāpī,\
sampajāno patissato”ti.[^1]

## Attribution

… Kosalavihāritthero ….[^2]

## Notes

[^1]: Variant: patissato”ti → paṭissatoti (mr)

[^2]: Variant: Kosalavihāritthero → kosallavihārī thero (sya1ed, sya2ed)
