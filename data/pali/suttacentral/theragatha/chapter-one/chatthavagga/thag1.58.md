# 1.58 Ramaṇīyakuṭika

“Ramaṇīyā me kuṭikā,\
Saddhādeyyā manoramā;\
Na me attho kumārīhi,\
Yesaṁ attho tahiṁ gacchatha nāriyo”ti.
