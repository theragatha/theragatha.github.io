# 1.60 Sīvali

“Te me ijjhiṁsu saṅkappā,\
yadattho pāvisiṁ kuṭiṁ;\
Vijjāvimuttiṁ paccesaṁ,\
mānānusayamujjahan”ti.

## Other

vaggo chaṭṭho.\

Tassuddānaṁ\

Godhiko ca subāhu ca,\
valliyo uttiyo isi;\
Añjanavaniyo thero,\
duve kuṭivihārino;\
Ramaṇīyakuṭiko ca,\
kosalavhayasīvalīti.
