# 1.98 Abhaya

“Rūpaṁ disvā sati muṭṭhā,\
Piyaṁ nimittaṁ manasikaroto;[^1]\
Sārattacitto vedeti,\
Tañca ajjhosa tiṭṭhati;\
Tassa vaḍḍhanti āsavā,\
Bhavamūlopagāmino”ti.[^2]

## Attribution

… Abhayo thero ….

## Notes

[^1]: Variant: Piyaṁ nimittaṁ → piyanimittaṁ (bj, sya1ed, sya2ed, pts1ed)

[^2]: Variant: Bhavamūlopagāmino”ti → bhavamūlā bhavagāminoti (bj, mr)
