# 1.94 Mettaji

“Namo hi tassa bhagavato,\
sakyaputtassa sirīmato;\
Tenāyaṁ aggappattena,\
aggadhammo sudesito”ti.[^1]

## Attribution

… Mettaji thero ….

[^1]: Variant: aggadhammo → aggo dhammo (bj)
