# 1.77 Hatthārohaputta

“Idaṁ pure cittamacāri cārikaṁ,\
Yenicchakaṁ yatthakāmaṁ yathāsukhaṁ;\
Tadajjahaṁ niggahessāmi yoniso,\
Hatthippabhinnaṁ viya aṅkusaggaho”ti.[^1]

… Hatthārohaputto thero ….

[^1]: Variant: Hatthippabhinnaṁ → hatthiṁ pabhinnaṁ (sya1ed, sya2ed, pts2ed)
