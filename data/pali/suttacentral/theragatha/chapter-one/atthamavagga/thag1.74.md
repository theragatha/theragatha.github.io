# 1.74 Suyāmana

“Kāmacchando ca byāpādo,\
Thinamiddhañca bhikkhuno;[^1]\
Uddhaccaṁ vicikicchā ca,\
Sabbasova na vijjatī”ti.

## Attribution

… Suyāmano thero ….

## Notes

[^1]: Variant: Thinamiddhañca → thīnamiddhañca (bj, sya1ed, sya2ed, pts1ed, pts2ed)
