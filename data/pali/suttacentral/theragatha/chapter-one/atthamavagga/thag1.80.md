# 1.80 Ugga

“Yaṁ mayā pakataṁ kammaṁ,\
appaṁ vā yadi vā bahuṁ;\
Sabbametaṁ parikkhīṇaṁ,\
natthi dāni punabbhavo”ti.

## Attribution

… Uggo thero …

## Notes

vaggo aṭṭhamo.

Tassuddānaṁ

Vacchapālo ca yo thero,
ātumo māṇavo isi;
Suyāmano susārado,
thero yo ca piyañjaho;
Ārohaputto meṇḍasiro,
rakkhito uggasavhayoti.
