# 1.73 Māṇava

“Jiṇṇañca disvā dukhitañca byādhitaṁ,\
Matañca disvā gatamāyusaṅkhayaṁ;\
Tato ahaṁ nikkhamitūna pabbajiṁ,\
Pahāya kāmāni manoramānī”ti.
