# 1.72 Ātuma

“Yathā kaḷīro susu vaḍḍhitaggo,\
Dunnikkhamo hoti pasākhajāto;[^1]\
Evaṁ ahaṁ bhariyāyānitāya,\
Anumaññaṁ maṁ pabbajitomhi dānī”ti.

## Attribution

… Ātumo thero ….

## Notes

[^1]: Variant: Dunnikkhamo → dunnikkhaso (sya1ed, sya2ed); dunnikkamo (pts2ed)
