# 1.30 Uttiyattheragāthā
“Ābādhe me samuppanne,
sati me udapajjatha;
Ābādho me samuppanno,
kālo me nappamajjitun”ti.

## Attribution

… Uttiyo thero …

## Other

vaggo tatiyo.

Tassuddānaṁ

Nigrodho cittako thero,
gosālathero sugandho;
Nandiyo abhayo thero,
thero lomasakaṅgiyo;
Jambugāmikaputto ca,
hārito uttiyo isīti.
