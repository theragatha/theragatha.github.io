# 1.27 Lomasakaṅgiya

“Dabbaṁ kusaṁ poṭakilaṁ,\
usīraṁ muñjapabbajaṁ;\
Urasā panudissāmi,[^1]\
vivekamanubrūhayan”ti.

## Attribution

… Lomasakaṅgiyo thero ….

## Notes

[^1]: Variant: panudissāmi → panudahissāmi (sya1ed, sya2ed, pts1ed)
