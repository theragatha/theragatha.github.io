# 1.25 Nandiyattheragāthā

“Obhāsajātaṁ phalagaṁ,\
cittaṁ yassa abhiṇhaso;\
Tādisaṁ bhikkhumāsajja,\
kaṇha dukkhaṁ nigacchasī”ti.
