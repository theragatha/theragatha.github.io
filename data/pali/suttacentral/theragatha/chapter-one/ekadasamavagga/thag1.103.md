# 1.103 Bandhura

“Nāhaṁ etena atthiko,\
Sukhito dhammarasena tappito;\
Pitvā rasaggamuttamaṁ,[^1]\
Na ca kāhāmi visena santhavan”ti.\

## Attribution

… Bandhuro thero ….[^2]

## Notes

[^1]: Variant: Pitvā → pitvāna (bj, sya1ed, sya2ed, pts2ed); pītvāna (pts1ed)

[^2]: Variant: Bandhuro → bandhano (mr)
