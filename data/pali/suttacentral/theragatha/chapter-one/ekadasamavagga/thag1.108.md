# 1.108 Dhammasavapitu

“Sa vīsavassasatiko,[^1]\
pabbajiṁ anagāriyaṁ;\
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,\
kataṁ buddhassa sāsanan”ti.

## Attribution

… Dhammasavapitu thero ….[^2]

## Notes

[^1]: Variant: vīsavassasatiko → sa vīsaṁ … (bj, sya1ed, sya2ed, pts2ed); savīsaṁ … (pts1ed)

[^2]: Variant: Dhammasavapitu → dhammasaṭapitu (sya1ed, sya2ed)
