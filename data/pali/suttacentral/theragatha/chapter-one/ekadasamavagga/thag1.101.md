# 1.101 Belaṭṭhānika

“Hitvā gihittaṁ anavositatto,[^1]\
Mukhanaṅgalī odariko kusīto;\
Mahāvarāhova nivāpapuṭṭho,\
Punappunaṁ gabbhamupeti mando”ti.

## Attribution

… Belaṭṭhāniko thero ….[^2]

## Notes

[^1]: Variant: gihittaṁ → gihitvaṁ (sya1ed, sya2ed, pts1ed)

[^2]: Variant: Belaṭṭhāniko → belaṭṭhakāni (sya1ed, sya2ed, pts1ed, pts2ed)
