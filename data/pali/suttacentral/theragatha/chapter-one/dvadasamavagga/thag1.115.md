# 1.115 Mahānāma

“Esāvahiyyase pabbatena,\
Bahukuṭajasallakikena;[^1]\
Nesādakena girinā,\
Yasassinā paricchadenā”ti.

## Attribution

… Mahānāmo thero ….

## Notes

[^1]: Variant: Bahukuṭajasallakikena → … sallarikena (bj, sya1ed, sya2ed)
