# 1.114 Adhimutta

“Kāyaduṭṭhullagaruno,\
hiyyamānamhi jīvite;[^1]\
Sarīrasukhagiddhassa,\
kuto samaṇasādhutā”ti.

## Attribution

… Adhimutto thero ….

## Notes

[^1]: Variant: hiyyamānamhi → hīyamānamhi (bj)
