# 1.85 Sunāga

“Cittanimittassa kovido,\
Pavivekarasaṁ vijāniya;[^1]\
Jhāyaṁ nipako patissato,\
Adhigaccheyya sukhaṁ nirāmisan”ti.

## Attribution

… Sunāgo thero ….

## Notes

[^1]: Variant: Pavivekarasaṁ → pavivekassa rasaṁ (pts2ed)
