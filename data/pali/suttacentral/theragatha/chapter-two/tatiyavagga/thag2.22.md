# 2.22 Bhaddaji

“Panādo nāma so rājā,\
yassa yūpo suvaṇṇayo;\
Tiriyaṁ soḷasubbedho,[^1]\
ubbhamāhu sahassadhā.[^2]

Sahassakaṇḍo satageṇḍu,[^3]\
dhajālu haritāmayo;\
Anaccuṁ tattha gandhabbā,\
chasahassāni sattadhā”ti.

## Attribution

… Bhaddajitthero ….

## Notes

[^1]: Variant: soḷasubbedho → soḷasapabbedho (bj, pts1ed, pts2ed); soḷasabbāṇo (?)

[^2]: Variant: ubbhamāhu → uddhamāhu (bj); uccamāhu (sya1ed, sya2ed)

[^3]: Variant: Sahassakaṇḍo → sahassakaṇḍu (pts1ed) | satageṇḍu → satabheṇḍu (bj, pts1ed)
