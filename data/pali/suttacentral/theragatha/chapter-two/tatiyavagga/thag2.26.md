# 2.26 Puṇṇamāsa

“Pañca nīvaraṇe hitvā,\
yogakkhemassa pattiyā;\
Dhammādāsaṁ gahetvāna,\
ñāṇadassanamattano.

Paccavekkhiṁ imaṁ kāyaṁ,\
sabbaṁ santarabāhiraṁ;\
Ajjhattañca bahiddhā ca,\
tuccho kāyo adissathā”ti.
