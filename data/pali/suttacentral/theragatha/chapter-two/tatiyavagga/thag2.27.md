# 2.27 Nandaka

“Yathāpi bhaddo ājañño,\
khalitvā patitiṭṭhati;\
Bhiyyo laddhāna saṁvegaṁ,\
adīno vahate dhuraṁ.

Evaṁ dassanasampannaṁ,\
sammāsambuddhasāvakaṁ;\
Ājānīyaṁ maṁ dhāretha,\
puttaṁ buddhassa orasan”ti.
