# 2.23 Sobhita

“Satimā paññavā bhikkhu,\
āraddhabalavīriyo;\
Pañca kappasatānāhaṁ,\
ekarattiṁ anussariṁ.

Cattāro satipaṭṭhāne,\
satta aṭṭha ca bhāvayaṁ;\
Pañca kappasatānāhaṁ,\
ekarattiṁ anussarin”ti.
