# 2.15 Sabbamitta

“Jano janamhi sambaddho,[^1]\
janamevassito jano;\
Jano janena heṭhīyati,\
heṭheti ca jano janaṁ.[^2]

Ko hi tassa janenattho,\
janena janitena vā;\
Janaṁ ohāya gacchaṁ taṁ,[^3]\
heṭhayitvā bahuṁ janan”ti.[^4]

## Attribution

… Sabbamitto thero ….

## Notes

[^1]: Variant: sambaddho → sambandho (sya1ed, sya2ed, mr)

[^2]: Variant: heṭheti ca → heṭheti ca (sya1ed, sya2ed, pts1ed, pts2ed); bādheti ca (mr)

[^3]: Variant: gacchaṁ taṁ → gaccheyya (sya1ed, sya2ed); gaccha taṁ (pts2ed)

[^4]: Variant: heṭhayitvā → bādhayitvā (mr)
