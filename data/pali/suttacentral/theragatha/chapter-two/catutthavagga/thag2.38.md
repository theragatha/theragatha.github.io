# 2.38 Nisabha

“Pañca kāmaguṇe hitvā,\
Piyarūpe manorame;\
Saddhāya gharā nikkhamma,[^1]\
Dukkhassantakaro bhave.

Nābhinandāmi maraṇaṁ,\
nābhinandāmi jīvitaṁ;\
Kālañca paṭikaṅkhāmi,\
sampajāno patissato”ti.
