# 2.13 Heraññakāni

Days & nights  
fly past.  
Life  
comes to an end.  
The span of mortals  
runs out,  
like the water of a piddling stream.  
But the fool doing evil deeds  
doesn’t realize that later  
it’s bitter for him:  
evil for him  
the result.
