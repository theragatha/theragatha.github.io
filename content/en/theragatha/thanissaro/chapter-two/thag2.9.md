# 2.9 Gotama

At ease sleep the sages  
who  
are not bound to women,  
in whom  
always to be protected  
is the truth  
very hard to gain.  
Sensuality,  
we’ve carried out  
your execution.  
No longer are we in your debt.  
We go now to unbinding  
where,  
having gone,  
one doesn’t  
grieve.
