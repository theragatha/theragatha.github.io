# 2.3 Valliya

A monkey,  
coming to the little hut  
with five doors,  
goes from door to door, knocking  
moment by moment.  
Stand still, monkey,  
don’t run.  
It’s not yours  
as it was before.  
You’re bound by discernment.  
You won’t get far away.
