## 2.6 Meḷajina  

When I heard the Teacher  
Speaking Dhamma,  
I wasn’t aware of any doubt  
In the all-knowing, unconquered one, 

The caravan leader, the great hero,  
The most excellent of charioteers.  
I have no doubt  
In the path or practice.
