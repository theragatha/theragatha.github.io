## 2.23 Sobhita

As a monk, mindful and wise,  
Resolute in power and energy,  
I recollected five hundred aeons  
In one night.  

Developing the four establishments of mindfulness,  
The seven factors of awakening,  
And the eightfold path,  
I recollected five hundred aeons  
In one night.
