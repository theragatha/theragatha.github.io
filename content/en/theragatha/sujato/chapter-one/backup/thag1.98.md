# 1.98 Abhaya  

If you focus on the pleasant aspect  
Of sights that you see, you’ll lose your mindfulness.  
Experiencing it with a lustful mind,  
You keep holding on.  
Your defilements grow,  
Leading to the root of rebirth  
In some state of existence.
