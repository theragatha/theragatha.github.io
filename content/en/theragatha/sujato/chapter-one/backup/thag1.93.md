# 1.93 Eraka

Sensual pleasures are suﬀering, Eraka!  
Sensual pleasures aren’t happiness, Eraka!  
One who enjoys sensual pleasures  
Enjoys suﬀering, Eraka!  
One who doesn’t enjoy sensual pleasures  
Doesn’t enjoy suﬀering, Eraka!
