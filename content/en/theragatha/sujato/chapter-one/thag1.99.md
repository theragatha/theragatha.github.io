# 1.99 Uttiya  

If you focus on the pleasant aspect  
Of sounds that you hear,  
You’ll lose your mindfulness.  
Experiencing it with a lustful mind,  
You keep holding on.  
Your defilements grow,  
Leading to transmigration.
