# 1.41 Sirivaḍḍha

The lightnings flash e'en in the rocky cave,\
Smiting Vebhāra's crest and Paṇḍava,\
And, in the mountain-bosom hid, a child\
Of that incomparable Master sits\
Ardent in contemplative ecstasy.
